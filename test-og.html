How to Test:

  1. Open this page in your browser
  2. Use Facebook's Sharing Debugger: https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/
  3. Use Twitter's Card Validator: https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator
  4. Or use LinkedIn's Post Inspector: https://www.linkedin.com/post-inspector/

For Production:

Replace http://localhost:3001 with https://swiper.fit in the meta tags.
